Far away in the future of the Galaxy, in the depths of the ancient Jedi archive, you have found a cozy reading nook. Here you delight yourself in perusing scrolls from bygone eras. One day, you stumble upon a parchment that reads on its dusty cover only the mysterious word "Legends"...
